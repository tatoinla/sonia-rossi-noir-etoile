THE ARTIST

Sonia Rossi
Chocolatier

Sonia Rossi is a visionary chocolatier whose creations transcend the boundaries of confectionery. Drawing from her Brazilian heritage and a lifelong devotion to health and beauty, she sculpts edible masterpieces that nourish both body and soul.

Every piece is crafted with organic, non-GMO, non-hydrogenated ingredients — free from artificial colors and preservatives. Sonia travels to Rome, London, São Paulo, Los Angeles, and Dallas, drawing inspiration from the world's most refined culinary traditions.

"I strive to craft confectionery creations that are both good for you and good for the planet."— Sonia Rossi

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Luxury Without
Compromise

🌿

Certified Organic

Every ingredient is sourced from certified organic farms. No pesticides, no shortcuts — only the purest cacao and natural flavors.

🧬

Non-GMO

We are committed to using only non-genetically modified ingredients, preserving the integrity of every flavor and the health of our clients.

✨

No Artificial Colors

Our stunning hues come exclusively from nature — butterfly pea flower, matcha, turmeric, and activated charcoal create our signature palette.

🌍

Sustainable Packaging

All packaging is earth-friendly and biodegradable, reflecting our commitment to the planet as much as to our clients' palates.
